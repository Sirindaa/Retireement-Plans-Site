What is Retirement and why do I need it?

Retirement is when you decide you want to stop working. This can occur at any age in your carreer. The average age of retirement in the US is 62. In order to live the rest of your life carelessly you must have money to do the things you want. This money is usually saved in a retirement plan. Though you can just save up money on your own, a retirement plan is usually the best route. There are lots of retirement plans available to you that help you manage your savings over the course of your career and can ultimately help you live the rest of your retired life in luxury.

This site was developed to help you decide which retirement plan is best suited for your needs and lifestyle. Look through the different retirement plans at the top of this page to get started!

The sooner you start your retirement plan the more money you'll ulitmately save up for the future!
